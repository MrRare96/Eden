Sword Art Online II

One year after the SAO incident, Kirito is approached by Seijiro Kikuoka from Japan's Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communications Department &quot;VR Division&quot; with a rather peculiar request.<br /> <br /> That was an investigation on the &quot;Death Gun&quot; incident that occurred in the gun and steel filled VRMMO called Gun Gale Online (GGO). &quot;Players who are shot by a mysterious avatar with a jet black gun lose their lives even in the real world...&quot; Failing to turn down Kikuoka's bizarre request, Kirito logs in to GGO even though he is not completely convinced that the virtual world could physically affect the real world.<br /> <br /> Kirito wanders in an unfamiliar world in order to gain any clues about the &quot;Death Gun.&quot; Then, a female sniper named Sinon who owns a gigantic &quot;Hecate II&quot; rifle extends Kirito a helping hand. With Sinon's help, Kirito decides to enter the &quot;Bullet of Bullets,&quot; a large tournament to choose the most powerful gunner within the realm of GGO, in hopes to become the target of the &quot;Death Gun&quot; and make direct contact with the mysterious avatar.<br /> <br /> (Source: Crunchyroll)
